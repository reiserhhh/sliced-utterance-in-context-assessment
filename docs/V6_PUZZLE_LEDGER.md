# V6 Puzzle Ledger: Measurement Closure

Updated 2026-07-14. This ledger separates estimator validation from psychological
interpretation. Estimator closure is mostly synthetic; real PANDORA positives are
restricted to numeric geometry and technical-slice robustness, never psychological
construct validity.

## Measurement objects

1. **Text path**: the realized conditional trajectory within one text. Licensed from one
   sufficiently long path; no author generalization.
2. **Author x context response**: `a_u + B_u z`. Licensed only with repeated occasions,
   observed opportunities, within-author condition excitation, and held-out conditions.
3. **Cross-context trait candidate**: a stable functional of `a_u`, `B_u`, recovery, or
   coupling. Not licensed until same-person free/fixed repeated human data pass
   invariance and matched-stranger gates.

## Binding refusal rules

- Missing opportunity or structural-zero indicators: do not separate response rate from
  expression opportunity.
- Rank-deficient within-author condition design: do not output an individual `B_u`.
- One occasion: report path coordinates only.
- Fewer than four windows: do not estimate window dynamics.
- If a corpus cannot supply the registered epoch x technical-replica design, do not
  attribute dynamic same-author signal to an author-level parameter rather than a
  persistent state.
- Unit root, long memory, unresolved low-frequency periodicity, or aliasing: do not apply
  short-memory aggregation claims.
- Hidden/rotatable latent conditions: report subspaces, not named axes.
- Failed identification is `REFUSE`; adequate identification with low power is
  `UNDECIDED`; estimator failure in an identified world is `FALSIFIED`.
- Discovery-side factor count is not a construct count. A factor family must
  transport as a subspace to author-disjoint confirmation data and its frozen
  scores must pass early/late reliability before any axis is named.
- A technically repeatable slice configuration is not a repeated occasion. Do
  not promote within-half technical-replica geometry to cross-context author or
  trait stability.
- Two temporal halves cannot identify direction, inhibition, recovery, or a
  person-specific response operator. Cross-block correspondence is undirected
  unless a repeated, independently perturbed design is available.
- Do not claim a hidden dynamic factor merely because broad distance ordering
  survives. The registered linear, path-signature, conditional-kernel, and
  fixed-grid diffusion alternatives are all insufficient in current PANDORA.

## Remaining bridge to psychological measurement

The missing evidence is one same-person design containing free text, fixed conditions,
at least three independent occasions, explicit opportunity metadata, and condition
support sufficient to estimate `B_u`. Only that design can test whether the recovered
author response object is stable across contexts and deserves trait-candidate status.
