# Report Authority

Reports named `QUICK` or `SMOKE` are development diagnostics. They are not
authoritative release evidence and may use reduced cohorts, reduced trials, or
earlier implementations. Corrected full reports, `docs/V7_DISCOVERY_LEDGER.md`,
and the tracked `release/v0.2.0/evidence/` snapshots take precedence whenever
numbers or statuses differ.

Aggregate reports contain no raw text or raw participant identifiers. Restricted
corpora and generated per-person artifacts remain excluded by `.gitignore`.
